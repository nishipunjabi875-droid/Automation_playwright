Gaps Identified & Upgrades

⚠️ Dynamic Shifting Gaps

Live Wooden Street Help Center uses expanding accordions. Expanding long answers pushes categories out of view, generating long scrolling offsets.

⚠️ Fragmented Escalation Channels

Support tools redirect shoppers away from the knowledge base (e.g. launching separate browser WhatsApp instances) reducing user retention.

⚠️ Static Answer Delivery

Search does not filter answers dynamically, forcing users to click through multiple topics to find relevant keywords.

✨ 3-Column Pane System

Adopts the Wakefit paradigm. The sidebar navigation filters the middle question column, and selecting a question displays the answer immediately on the right panel.

✨ In-page Conversational Woody Bot

Shoppers can check refund timing, order location, or wood quality without ever leaving the page, via simulated quick action buttons or typed inputs.

✨ Embedded Ticket & Track Modals

Direct overlays let users track order Jaipurs shipments or submit issue details immediately.
